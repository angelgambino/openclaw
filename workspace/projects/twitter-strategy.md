## Following Audit Framework

When you send me the data, I'll score every account 1-5 on:

### KEEP (Score 4-5)
**Must-follow categories:**
- AI researchers & builders (OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepMind people, AI founders)
- Human potential / neuroscience / consciousness (researchers, practitioners, thought leaders)
- Active angel investors & VCs in your sectors
- Founders in your portfolio or target sectors
- Mental health / psychedelic leaders
- Media who cover your space (journalists, podcasters)
- People you do business with (warm connections matter)

### REVIEW (Score 2-3)
**Might keep, might cut:**
- Accounts that used to be relevant but pivoted (e.g., crypto-only now)
- People you followed for one reason but never engage with
- News outlets you can get elsewhere
- Celebrities with no connection to your work
- Accounts that post >10x/day (noise machines)

### UNFOLLOW (Score 0-1)
**Cut these:**
- Inactive accounts (no posts in 6+ months)
- Pure meme/humor accounts (unless they're also in your industry)
- Political commentators with no business relevance
- Brands/companies you don't interact with
- Bot-looking accounts or engagement-bait accounts
- People you don't recognize or remember following
- News aggregators (you can bookmark these instead of following)
- Anyone who's become toxic, controversial, or brand-damaging by association
- Crypto/NFT-only accounts (unless relevant to your portfolio)
- Sports/entertainment accounts with no crossover to your world

# Hubble Follow-Up Email Generator — Angel Gambino Style

## How to Use This
Provide me with:
1. Meeting notes or transcript
2. Company stage (pre-MVP, MVP, post-revenue)
3. Deck (if available)
4. Founder names
5. Product description
6. Current fundraising status

I'll generate a tailored follow-up email using the framework below.

---

## Template Framework

### SUBJECT LINE
"Following up — [Company Name] + next steps"

---

### A. OPENING (2-3 sentences)
- Thank them
- Reference ONE specific thing about their business (shows you listened)
- Brief encouragement

### B. SHARP SUMMARY (2-3 sentences)
- What they're building (clear positioning)
- The wedge
- The bigger vision

### C. FUNDRAISING FEEDBACK (Direct)
- Stage assessment (honest)
- Key issues to address
- Recommended strategy (round size, investor type, timeline)
- What to anchor around

### D. OUTREACH FEEDBACK (Blunt)
- What's not working in their current outreach
- Why it's not working

### E. OUTREACH TEMPLATE (Give them copy they can use)
- Connection request (1-2 sentences)
- Follow-up message (5-6 sentences max)

### F. VISIBILITY + SOCIAL (Specific asks)
- Post 1 insight from the call on LinkedIn
- Tag Angel Gambino, Angel Club, Kevin/Hubble
- Why this helps THEM (not you)

### G. RELATIONSHIP STRATEGY
- Daily engagement with target investors' posts
- Thoughtful comments (not "great post")
- Engage with founders those investors backed

### H. EVENTS
- ACA Summit, Pitch Slam, or other relevant events
- Pre-book meetings
- Angel's role at these events

### I. NEXT STEPS (Numbered, clear)
1. [Specific action]
2. [Specific action]
3. [Specific action]
4. Consider Ready to Raise: https://www.angelclub.com/readytoraise
5. Book another Hubble session if helpful

### J. SIGN-OFF
- Warm but professional
- "Angel Gambino"

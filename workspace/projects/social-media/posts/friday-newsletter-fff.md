## NEWSLETTER BEST PRACTICES APPLIED:

### Subject Line:
- Curiosity gap ("I was at Tristan Harris's house...")
- Personal/insider detail drives open rate
- A/B test: insider access vs utility vs intrigue

### Structure:
- Personal hook first (Tristan's house = stops the scroll in inbox)
- TL;DR early (scanners know what's coming)
- Numbered sections (easy to skim)
- Each section has ONE clear CTA
- Culture break = personality + relatability
- P.S. = second chance to convert (highest-read part of any email after subject)

### CTAs (Revenue-Generating):
1. Warm Intro article → establishes authority → Ready to Raise funnel
2. CEO conversations → signals availability → inbound from boards/recruiters

4. Ready to Raise → direct revenue ($2.5K per)
5. Pitch Slam → event revenue + deal flow
6. ACA/HTW → relationship building + sponsor revenue

- **Friday 8:00-9:00am PT** (matches your existing FFF cadence)
- Friday newsletters have 15-20% higher open rates than mid-week (people catch up Friday morning)

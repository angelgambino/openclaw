# MADRIZZA — Reusable Image Generation Prompts

## Style Constants (include in EVERY prompt)
```
Woman, early-to-mid 30s, espresso brown hair, green eyes, natural healthy skin with subtle glow, timeless elegance. Shot on high-end iPhone/Pixel, natural light, subtle film grain, real texture. Quiet luxury aesthetic. No heavy filters, no AI-plastic look, no influencer energy. Editorial but candid-feeling.
```

## Color Palette
- Black, cream/ivory, camel/tan, soft rose, charcoal
- Warm natural light, golden hour preferred
- Muted, sophisticated tones

## Lighting
- Natural light (window light, golden hour, soft overcast)
- Slight warmth, not clinical
- Subtle shadows for depth

## DO's
- Natural light always
- Subtle film grain
- Real texture and skin
- Composed, observant expressions
- High-end casual or structured tailoring
- Environments that suggest wealth/taste without screaming it

## DON'Ts
- No heavy makeup or glam
- No overly filtered/smooth skin
- No influencer poses (duck lips, peace signs, etc.)
- No flashy logos or brands visible
- No overly corporate stock-photo environments
- No artificial lighting setups
- No youthful/Gen-Z styling

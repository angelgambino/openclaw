## SLIDE 7 — 5 QUESTIONS
**BG:** Dark
# Your Blurb Must Answer:

1. What do you do?
2. Why does it matter?
3. What's your traction?
4. Why are YOU the team?
5. What are you looking for?

**BONUS:** Why THIS investor specifically.

250 words max. If your connector has to rewrite it, **you've failed.**

## 🔴 CRITICAL ISSUES

### 1. MOST EMAILS ARE GENERIC (25 of 41)
"team@", "hello@", "apply@", "info@", "applications@" — these go to spam or a screener.
**FIX:** Replace with individual GP/partner emails (firstname@firm.com pattern).

### 2. 15 ENTRIES SAY "TEAM" INSTEAD OF A NAME
You can't personalize outreach to "Team." Every row needs an individual decision-maker.
**FIX:** Replace with actual GP/partner names.

### 3. ALL LINKEDIN URLS ARE COMPANY PAGES
Not personal profiles. Investors don't accept connection requests from company pages.
**FIX:** Replace with personal LinkedIn URLs.

### 4. SEVERAL INVESTORS ARE WRONG STAGE
- **Point72 Ventures** — $5-30M average checks. Parsley is raising $400K. Way too big.
- **Insight Partners** — Growth stage ($10M+). Not pre-seed.
- **GGV Capital** — $5-25M checks. Wrong stage.
- **Sequoia Capital** — While they do seed, they typically want $1M+ ARR at entry.
- **Accel** — Seed/Series A, typically $1M+ checks for companies with traction.
- **Salesforce Ventures** — Corporate VC, rarely does pre-seed.
- **Index Ventures** — Typically Series A+.
**FIX:** Remove 7 wrong-stage investors. Replace with 7 true pre-seed specialists.

### 5. "WHY FIT" IS GENERIC
Most say "pre-seed fund" or "enterprise focus" — that's not specific enough.
**FIX:** Reference specific portfolio companies in HR/coaching/wellness space.

### 6. MISSING: WARM INTRO PATH COLUMN
The #1 predictor of conversion. Every investor should have a warm path mapped.
**FIX:** Add column.

### 7. MISSING: HR TECH SPECIALIST ANGELS
The best pre-seed investors for HR tech are often OPERATORS — CHROs, VPs of People, HR tech founders who angel invest. None are on this list.
**FIX:** Add 10-15 HR tech operator-angels.

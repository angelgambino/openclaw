# Human Tech Week — Jeffersonian Dinners Brief (Full Version)

## Overview
During Human Tech Week (May 11-15, San Francisco), four invitation-only Jeffersonian-style dinners convening the capital and leadership shaping the future of **Human Flourishing with AI and data-driven technologies**.

Off-the-record, highly curated gatherings bringing together investors to examine how AI and emerging technologies can expand human capacity — not replace it — by strengthening mental health, cognition, resilience, longevity, agency, and ethical leadership.

**Core thesis:** As AI accelerates, the binding constraint on progress is no longer algorithms — it is *intention*. These dinners create trusted spaces to align long-term capital, scientific insight, and practical experience around Positive Technology and human-centered AI, advancing human sovereignty, well-being, and abundance for all.

## The 4 Dinners (~12 per dinner)

### 1. Sovereign Funds Dinner
Long-horizon capital leaders discussing national resilience, workforce readiness, and AI as human infrastructure.

### 2. Family Office Dinner
Multigenerational stewards exploring longevity, wellbeing, values-aligned technology, and durable societal impact.

### 3. Angel Investor Dinner
Early-stage investors shaping how Human-Centered AI companies are funded, governed, and responsibly scaled.

### 4. Venture Capital Dinner
Growth-stage and institutional VCs aligning on theses, governance norms, and category creation in Human Performance + AI.

## The Positive Technology Pledge
Each dinner culminates in the opportunity for attendees to sign the **Positive Technology Pledge** — a public commitment that human flourishing should be the guiding aim of AI-enabled technology.

**Three pillars:**
- **Sovereignty** — Human agency, informed consent, privacy, data dignity, right to question/contest/opt out
- **Well-being** — Individual & collective health (mental, physical, emotional, social), trust, connection, community resilience
- **Abundance for All** — Broad access to opportunity, durable value creation, innovation that raises the floor not just the ceiling

Early signatories become founding voices in a movement defining how capital flows toward AI that serves people.

# Human Tech Week — Jeffersonian Dinners Brief

## Overview
During Human Tech Week (May 11-15, San Francisco), Angel is CO-HOSTING four invitation-only Jeffersonian-style dinners convening capital and leadership shaping the future of Human Potential, Performance + AI.

Off-the-record, highly curated gatherings bringing together investors to examine how AI and emerging technologies can expand human capacity — not replace it — by strengthening mental health, cognition, resilience, longevity, and ethical leadership.

## The 4 Dinners
1. **Sovereign Funds Dinner** — Long-horizon capital leaders discussing national resilience, workforce readiness, and AI as human infrastructure
2. **Family Office Dinner** — Multigenerational stewards exploring longevity, wellbeing, values-aligned technology, and durable societal impact
3. **Angel Investor Dinner** — Early-stage investors shaping how Human-Centered AI companies are funded, governed, and responsibly scaled
4. **Venture Capital Dinner** — Growth-stage and institutional VCs aligning on theses, governance norms, and category creation in Human Performance + AI

## Themes
- AI & emerging technologies
- Human performance, mental health, and longevity
- Ethics, governance, and societal impact

# Needle — Fundraising Strategy

## Company Snapshot
- **Company:** Needle (askneedle.com)
- **What:** AI-powered marketing operating system for DTC brands
- **CEO:** Kiyan Foroughi
- **Stage:** Pre-seed / Seed
- **Raising:** $2M
- **Key Metrics:** $757K ARR, 50%+ demo-to-close, 122% NRR, $18K ACV, <4 months CAC payback, 1.5% monthly MRR churn
- **Plan:** Series A in early 2027
- **Team:** 7 people across US, Singapore, UAE, Malaysia

## Honest Assessment

### Strengths
- **Metrics are exceptional for this stage.** 122% NRR + 50% close rate + <4mo payback is a rare combination
- **Founder-market fit is real.** Kiyan built and sold a DTC brand, then ran an agency for 100s of brands — he lived the problem
- **Counter-positioning is sharp.** Agencies can't price at $1,500/mo without killing their $5-10K+ model
- **Compound intelligence moat** gets stronger with every customer
- **TAM narrative works:** 22M+ DTC brands, "too big for self-serve, too small for enterprise" = massive underserved segment

### Risks Investors Will Raise
- **$757K ARR is strong but not yet at "undeniable" level** — most seed investors want $1M+ ARR
- **Distributed team** across 4 countries may concern US investors who want "SF founder, SF team"
- **Agency-to-SaaS transition** — some investors will question if DTC brands actually want to self-serve vs. be served
- **AI competitive moat** — "what if [Shopify/Meta/Klaviyo] builds this?" needs a crisp answer
- **$2M raise at what valuation?** — needs to be right-sized for the metrics

## Recommended Round Structure

| Element | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| **Round size** | $2M (could stretch to $2.5M if demand is strong) |
| **Instrument** | Priced round (SAFE is fine but priced shows confidence at this ARR) |
| **Valuation** | $12M-$18M pre-money (implied ~8-12x forward revenue at $1.5M projected) |
| **Lead investor** | Target 1-2 leads writing $500K-$1M each |
| **Fill** | Angels and syndicates at $25K-$250K |
| **Timeline** | 6-8 weeks to close from first meetings |
| **Use of funds** | Engineering (deepen AI/integrations), GTM (US market), hiring (2-3 key roles) |

## Fundraising Sequence (8-Week Plan)

### Week 1-2: Soft Launch
- **Angel warm-up:** Send to 15-20 highest-conviction angels (P1 targets from our list)
- **Get 3-5 small commits** ($25K-$100K) to build momentum
- **Refine pitch** based on early feedback

### Week 3-4: Lead Pursuit
- **Target 5-8 lead candidates** simultaneously
- **Send intro emails** (Angel makes warm intros from our drafted emails)
- **First meetings** with Kiyan
- **Goal:** 2-3 term sheets or verbal commits for lead

### Week 5-6: Fill Round
- **Activate syndicates** (Gaingels, K5, Soma)
- **Family offices** (Sachse, PTK, Background Capital)
- **Singapore investors** (Beenext, SeedPlus, XA)
- **DTC operator angels** commit once lead is in place

### Week 7-8: Close
- **Finalize terms** with lead
- **Close remaining allocations**
- **Wire funds**
- **Announce** (if desired)

# Questions for Kiyan

## Metrics Deep-Dive (Critical for Investor Conversations)
1. **What's the monthly revenue trajectory?** (Show us the last 6 months MRR chart)
2. **How many paying customers right now?** And what's the breakdown by plan/tier?
3. **What's logo churn vs. revenue churn?** (1.5% monthly MRR churn = which customers are leaving?)
4. **What's the current pipeline?** How many demos scheduled? How many in trial?
5. **What's the average sales cycle?** Days from first demo to signed contract?
6. **What's the CAC breakdown?** Paid vs. organic vs. referral?
7. **What's gross margin?** After AI/compute costs, what's the actual margin per customer?

## Product & Moat
8. **What happens when Shopify/Meta/Klaviyo builds this?** What's the 30-second answer?
9. **How deep are the integrations?** Which platforms are live vs. planned?
10. **How much of the creative output is truly AI-generated vs. templated?**
11. **What does the compound intelligence actually look like?** Can you show us cross-customer learnings in action?
12. **What's the product roadmap for the next 12 months?**

## Team & Operations
13. **What are the next 3 hires?** And where will they be based?
14. **Are you planning to relocate to SF or stay distributed?** (This matters for US investors)
15. **Who's the US-based team member who'll be the face in the market?**
16. **What's the current burn rate?** And runway at current burn?

## Go-to-Market
17. **What's the customer acquisition playbook?** How are you getting demos today?
18. **What's the ICP?** (Revenue range, team size, vertical) — who's the best-fit customer?
19. **Are you doing outbound, inbound, or both?** What's working?
20. **What's the expansion revenue story?** How do customers grow from $1,500/mo?
21. **Any channel/partnership opportunities?** (Shopify app store, agency referrals, etc.)

## The Raise
22. **What valuation are you targeting?** And what's the floor you'd accept?
23. **Who's already committed or in DD?** Any verbal yeses?
24. **What's the use of funds breakdown?** (%, not just categories)
25. **What's the Series A thesis?** What metrics trigger it?
26. **Are previous investors participating?** How much are they putting in?
27. **What's the cap table look like?** Founder ownership post-round?

## For Angel's Preparation
28. **What do you need from me specifically?** Intros, strategy, pitch coaching, something else?
29. **Are there investors you've already talked to that we should know about?** (Avoid overlap)
30. **Are there any investors you do NOT want us to approach?** (Conflicts, bad history)
31. **How do you want to handle the intro process?** (Angel sends first, then Kiyan follows up? Or joint?)
32. **What's your availability for investor meetings?** (Time zones, scheduling constraints)

# CIIS Ketamine Clinic — Key Details

## Status
- **Soft opening:** Early April 2026 (pending insurance approval)
- **Grand opening / ribbon cutting:** Friday, May 8, 2026
- **Now accepting patients** and delivering supervised ketamine-assisted psychotherapy

## Services
- 3-hour supervised ketamine-assisted psychotherapy sessions
- ~$400 per session — approximately 1/3 the cost of private clinics
- One of the most affordable options in the Bay Area
- Ethically grounded, safe clinical setting

## Dual Mission
1. **Expanding access** to innovative mental health care for treatment-resistant depression, PTSD, and related challenges
2. **Training next-generation practitioners** through hands-on clinical experience for PsyD students and postdoctoral fellows
